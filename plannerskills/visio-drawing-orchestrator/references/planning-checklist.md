# Planning Checklist

## Before Drawing

1. Identify the diagram family.
2. Identify major regions and lanes.
3. Separate semantic nodes from decorative containers.
4. Separate always-visible labels from optional annotations.
5. Note typography and line-style hierarchy.

## Before Handing Off To drawskills

1. Confirm font default.
2. Confirm whether text-box coupling matters strongly.
3. Confirm whether rotated text is required.
4. Confirm minimum spacing expectations.
5. Confirm whether side panels or legends should be drawn independently.

## Before Handing Off To visioskills

1. Confirm required primitive operations exist.
2. Confirm preview export is enabled.
3. Confirm save/close behavior follows job config.
